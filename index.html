What is The Loop?

A place to actually get better at Zouk DJing.

Most DJ communities are either too casual — vibes, no substance — or too gatekeep-y. The Loop is neither.

It's a focused space for Brazilian Zouk DJs who want real growth: building skills, sharing music, getting thoughtful feedback, and connecting with other DJs who understand what it means to read a dance floor.

Whether you've been spinning for a month or a decade, there's something here for you. We're all learners. That's the whole point.

Every level
From complete beginners to seasoned selectors — no gatekeeping, just growth. Zouk-specific from day one.
Real feedback
Share your Zouk mixes, get honest and constructive responses from people who actually know the craft.
Curated community
Quality over quantity — a structured community keeps the informational value at maximum.

What you get

Everything a growing Zouk DJ needs.

🎧

Mix Feedback

Drop a Zouk mix or a transition, get real ears on it. Structure, energy, track selection, floor dynamics — we help you hear what you can't hear yourself.

📚

Shared Knowledge

Zouk-specific tips, musicality guides, gear questions, software — a library of knowledge built by the community, for the community.

Music Discovery

Who we are

DJs building for DJs.

We're a small group of Brazilian Zouk DJs who met through the music and decided to build the community we wished existed when we were starting out. Three DJs, three countries, one shared obsession.

DJ Kron

Czech Republic

DJ Kron is a Brazilian Zouk DJ, dancer, and teacher from the Czech Republic. Known for deep, emotional sets that challenge dancers' creativity and build musical flow, he's been shaping Zouk floors across Europe for over 8 years. A lifelong music lover who believes the right track at the right moment can change a room.

DJ Sprenk

USA

DJ Sprenk is a Brazilian Zouk DJ based in Cambridge, MA. Originally a musician, his approach to DJing is rooted in music theory and emotional storytelling — known across North America for genre-blending arcs and a healthy dose of nerdy joy.

DJ Xearo

Netherlands

DJ Xearo is a DJ, musical director, and instructor working internationally within the Brazilian Zouk community. Based in Amsterdam and playing top marathons and festivals across Europe since 2016, his focus is always the same: right music, right moment.
