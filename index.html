What is The Loop?

A place to actually get better

Most DJ communities are either too casual — vibes, no substance — or too gatekeep-y. The Loop is neither. It's a focused space for people who want real growth: building skills, sharing music, getting thoughtful feedback, and connecting with other DJs who take the craft seriously.

Whether you've been spinning for a month or a decade, there's something here for you. We're all learners. That's the whole point.

Every level
From bedroom beginners to seasoned selectors — no gatekeeping, just growth
Real feedback
Share your mixes, get honest and constructive responses from people who care
Curated community
Quality over quantity — early access keeps the signal-to-noise ratio high

Who we are

DJs building for DJs

We're a small group of DJs who met through the music and decided to build the community we wished existed when we were starting out.

DJ Kron

DJ Kron is a Zouk DJ, teacher, dancer and music producer. He loves to spread positive energy and connect people through his music.

DJ Sprenk

DJ Sprenk is a Brazilian Zouk DJ based out of Cambridge, MA, USA, known for musicality and energetic flow. With a background in performing and arranging music, their approach to DJing is deeply rooted in music theory and emotional storytelling.

DJ Xearo

DJ Xearo is a DJ, musical director, and DJ instructor working internationally within the Brazilian Zouk community and beyond. At the heart of his work is a simple belief: right music, right moment. Whether behind the decks, shaping the musical direction of an event, or working with other DJs, his focus is always the same — creating moments where music feels natural, intentional, and alive in the body.
